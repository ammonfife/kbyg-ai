## ğŸ’¡ Content Tips

### Headlines
- **Instagram**: 6-8 words, punchy
- **Twitter**: 8-10 words, informative
- **LinkedIn**: 10-12 words, professional
- **Story**: 3-5 words, HUGE

### Descriptions
- **Instagram**: 1 sentence, benefit-focused
- **Twitter**: 2 sentences, data-driven
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3 sentences, detailed
- **Story**: Very brief or none

### CTAs
- **Instagram**: "Learn More", "Discover", "Get Started"
- **Twitter**: "Read More", "Learn How", "Try Now"
- **LinkedIn**: "Download", "Register", "Connect"
- **Story**: "Swipe Up", "Tap", "Click Link"

## ğŸš€ Pro Tips

1. **Test on mobile** - 80% of social media is mobile
2. **Use contrast** - Ensure text is readable
3. **Keep it simple** - One message per graphic
4. **Brand consistency** - Use same colors/fonts
5. **A/B test** - Try different headlines
6. **Add urgency** - Limited time, exclusive, new
7. **Include CTA** - Always tell them what to do
8. **Optimize images** - Use high-quality backgrounds
9. **Preview first** - Check all platforms
10. **Save versions** - Keep source files
