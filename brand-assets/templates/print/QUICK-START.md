## Print Recommendations

### Business Cards
- **Vendor:** Moo.com or VistaPrint
- **Paper:** 16pt cardstock, matte finish
- **Quantity:** Start with 250-500

### Letterhead
- **Vendor:** PrintPlace or Overnight Prints
- **Paper:** 24lb white bond, uncoated
- **Quantity:** 500-1000 sheets

### Flyers
- **Vendor:** UPrinting or local print shop
- **Paper:** 100lb gloss text
- **Quantity:** 250-1000

### Posters
- **Vendor:** Signs.com or Printful
- **Material:** 10mil poster paper or vinyl
- **Mounting:** Foam board for trade shows

**Q: Colors look different on screen vs print**  
A: This is normal! Request a color proof from your printer.

**Q: Fonts don't load**  
A: Make sure you have internet connection (Google Fonts). Or install Inter font locally.

**Q: PDF too large**  
A: Compress images to 300 DPI max. Use JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics.
