Supabase requires email confirmation before you can sign in. Test emails won't work.

üîß Quick Fix Options:

Option 1: Disable Email Confirmation (Recommended for Testing)

  1. Go to: Supabase Auth Settings
  2. Click Email provider
  3. Toggle OFF: "Confirm email"
  4. Click Save
  5. Try signing up again in extension

Option 2: Use Google OAuth (Best Option)

  1. See GOOGLE_OAUTH_READY.md for setup
  2. Configure Google OAuth in Supabase
  3. Use "Continue with Google" button

Option 3: Use a Real Email

  1. Sign up with your actual email (e.g., ammonfife@gmail.com)
  2. Check your inbox for confirmation email
  3. Click the link to confirm
  4. Sign in with the extension

Option 4: Anonymous Mode (No Auth)

Just skip sign in - the extension works in anonymous mode! Data saves locally.

Recommended: Disable email confirmation in Supabase for now, or use Google OAuth.
